Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import my existing hymn list?

Choose File › Import and select either a plain‑text (.txt) or JSON (.json) file. A preview lets you pick which hymns to keep and resolves duplicates automatically.

Can I project verses and chorus separately?

Yes. Tag each section in the editor and Church Hymn will split slides at the right points during presentation.

Does Church Hymn work offline?

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your Mac, so you can use the app without an internet connection.
