Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 August 2025

1. Introduction

Church Hymn is an offline macOS application that helps churches display hymn lyrics. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your data safe. This policy explains what limited data we handle and how we handle it.

2. Data We Don’t Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, address, etc.).
  • No analytics or usage tracking.
  • No advertising identifiers.

3. Data Stored Locally

Any hymns you create, import, or edit are stored only on your Mac inside the App Sandbox. They never leave your device unless you choose to export or share them.

4. File Access Permissions

When you import or export files, macOS asks you to select the file or folder. Church Hymn receives read/write access only to the locations you approve through this system dialog.

5. Third‑Party Services

Church Hymn uses no third‑party SDKs, cloud services, or external APIs.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for adult administrators and worship leaders. It does not target children under 13, collects no personal data, and complies with COPPA.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, email paulobfsilva@gmail.com.
